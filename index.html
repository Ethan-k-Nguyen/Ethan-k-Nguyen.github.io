Delicious Meatloaf Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup ketchup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, onion, green pepper, milk, egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Press mixture into a loaf pan.
  4. Invert pan contents onto a sheet trap.
  5. Spread ketchup over the top.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
